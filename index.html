Foundation stone of our firm- Dudheshwar Engineers, laid down back in the year 1990. Since then, we are meeting market needs as a manufacturer, & Exporter of all type of earthmoving spares like JCB Spare Parts, Heavy Earth Moving Spare Parts, Earth moving bearings, Pin bush, Piston, Head end cover, Bushes like, Gun Metal bush, Brass bush, Aluminium-Bronze bush, S.S bush, SGI bush, Bronze bush, Casting bush, Bi-metal bush & as per customer requirement in any grade & also making quality all type industrial needle roller bearings to the customers as per their specific needs. All our products are offered to the honorable patrons in varied sizes, grades, dimensions, shapes and specifications with the aim to meet diverse application requirements. Owing to special features like precision-engineering, durability, dimensional accuracy and tough construction, our offered spare parts are highly demanded by the customers in the market.
